# E-Commerce Business Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the Brazilian Olist E-Commerce dataset using Microsoft SQL Server and Microsoft Excel. The objective is to evaluate business performance by analyzing revenue, customer behavior, product performance, and operational efficiency. SQL was used to answer key business questions, and the results were visualized through an interactive Excel dashboard.

---

##  Project Objectives

- Analyze overall business performance.
- Identify monthly revenue trends.
- Evaluate customer purchasing behavior.
- Identify top-performing product categories.
- Monitor delivery performance.
- Measure customer satisfaction using review scores.
- Provide actionable business recommendations.

##  Business Questions Answered

- What is the total revenue generated?

##  Key Business Insights

- Revenue showed consistent growth before stabilizing toward the end of the analysis period.
- A small number of product categories contributed a significant share of total revenue.
- The repeat customer rate is relatively low, highlighting an opportunity to improve customer retention.
- Approximately 92% of orders were delivered on time, while delayed deliveries present an opportunity for operational improvement.
- The average review score of 4.07/5 indicates strong customer satisfaction.

---

##  Business Recommendations

- Introduce customer loyalty programs to improve repeat purchases.
- Reduce delivery delays by optimizing logistics operations.
- Increase investment in high-performing product categories.
- Improve underperforming product categories through pricing and promotional strategies.
- Continue monitoring KPIs using interactive dashboards for data-driven decision-making.
